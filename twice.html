트와이스.

Twice is a South Korean girl group formed by JYP Entertainment. The group is composed of nine members: Nayeon, Jeongyeon, Momo, Sana, Jihyo, Mina, Dahyun, Chaeyoung, and Tzuyu. Twice was formed under the television program Sixteen (2015) and debuted on October 20, 2015, with the extended play (EP) The Story Begins.

Their debut happened during the start of the '3rd gen' era, quickly making their way up the charts with their bubbly pop songs and charming smiles. Their 'cute' concept took the world by storm, forming the foundation of what would be a very loyal fandom who grew with them, encouraging the girl group to experiment with 'girl crush' and 'darker' concepts as their discography grew.

The girls' chemistry, charm and talent led to them being dubbed "the Nation's girl group", their differently textured voices and ability to shine without outshining each other drawing people in. Nayeon's smile and Jeongyeon's witty humor to Tzuyu's calm but sarcastic aura and Mina's cool exterior, it's without a doubt that these girls were meant to be together as a group, as a family, and take on the world together

Nayeon (임나연) - represented by the bunny emoji (which is very fitting, i must say), Nayeon is every bit the Virgo the stars say she is. Her charisma makes all her fancams and performances eye-catching and never boring, and we can really see her shine in her solo debut, "IM NAYEON".

Jeongyeon (정연) - when you love Jeongyeon there is no exit - if that isn't the truest thing I've ever heard! Her charm is in the jokes and laughter she fills every room with. One of the most talented vocalists in the industry, her enchanting voice belting out notes on stage while performing difficult choreography is truly one of the most fascinating things to see.

Momo (모모) - the oldest of twice's j-line, Momo's beauty and dancing skills are no joke. Her doll like features and fluid movements leave you breathless, and her smile glitters like a thousand diamonds.

Sana (사나) - our clumsy ball of sunshine, they didn't lie when they said 'No Sana, No Life'. Her larger than life personality and doe eyes are sure to make everyone fall in love with her within 5 minutes. The unique tone to her voice adds to the original 'cute' concept of twice - like it was made for her

Jihyo (지효) - the headstrong, smiley leader, Jihyo's stage presence and smile will draw you in with every performance. Whenever you watch a video of all of them, you can't help but keep looking back towards Jihyo. She's like a giant teddy bear too - always ready to protect her members no matter what, and so incredibly huggable. Who doesn't love Park Jihyo?

Mina (미나) - Mina - forever graceful, loveable, beautiful Mina. Represented by the penguin emoji (very fitting - have you seen her penguin walk? :,) I could talk about it for weeks), Myoui Mina used to be a ballerina before she joined the kpop industry. Her soothing, enchanting voice and cool and efficient movements leave you absolutely mesmerised.

Dahyun (다현) - Tofu Dahyun with the superpower of finding cameras no matter how hidden they are, her charming smile and enchanting winks add color to all their performances. One of the funniest twice members (no kidding - she will leave you clutching at your stomach, rolling over laughing), she definitely has one of the most beautiful souls.

Chaeyoung (채영) - the definition of a true artist, Chaeyoung's pen (whether in writing songs or drawing) isn't something to be underestimated. Loved by fans because of her chill personality and cool vibes, Chaeyoung is definitely the one to convince you to stay and love Twice forever.

Tzuyu (쯔위) - calm, sarcastic and witty Tzuyu who never fails to make her unnies melt. Her charm lies in the quick one-liners she drops unexpectedly, leaving everyone shocked for a few seconds before laughing. One of the more introverted members of the group, Tzuyu is content to just watch and laugh at all her members, her family. Her soft voice and calm movements make her fancams soothing to watch - it leaves you mesmerised at her beauty as well
